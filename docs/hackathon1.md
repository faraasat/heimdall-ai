1. Hosting & Backend (Free / Generous Free Tiers)
   Compute & APIs

Vercel
Best for frontend + serverless APIs. Zero config, fast deploys, great for demos.

Netlify
Similar to Vercel. Works well for static sites and simple functions.

Railway
Easy backend hosting for Node, Python, Postgres, Redis.

Render
Good for APIs, background jobs, and databases with free tiers.

Excellent for edge APIs, auth gateways, and fast global demos.

Databases

Supabase
Postgres + Auth + Storage. Ideal for hackathons.

Neon

Firebase (Firestore + Auth)
